Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2024

1. Introduction

RingosDate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

By using RingosDate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and date of birth
  • Email address
  • Profile photos
  • Gender and preferences
  • Bio and profile information

Location Data:

  • Your location when using the app
  • Transit route selections (S-Bahn, U-Bahn, Tram)
  • Departure times and stations

Usage Data:

  • Swipe history and matches
  • Messages sent through the app
  • Event participation
  • App interaction data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Match you with other users on the same transit route
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Send push notifications about matches and messages
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Other users (profile information, as you choose to share)
  • Firebase/Google (for authentication and data storage)
  • VBB API (for transit departure times - no personal data)

Your messages are only visible to you and the recipient.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • Profile photos are stored in Firebase Storage
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, use the Settings menu in the app or contact us.

7. Location Data

We collect location data only when you actively use the app. This data is used to:

  • Show your distance from other users
  • Enable location-based matching

You can disable location access in your device settings.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:

  • New matches
  • New messages
  • Event requests

You can disable notifications in your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

RingosDate is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
