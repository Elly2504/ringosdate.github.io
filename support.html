Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ringos work?

Ringos connects you with people taking the same public transit at the same time. Select your S-Bahn, U-Bahn, or Tram line, choose your station and departure time, and see who else is on your ride!

Is Ringos free to use?

Ringos is free to download with 20 free swipes per day. Upgrade to Ringos+ for unlimited swipes and premium features starting at ‚Ç¨4.99/month.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data including profile, photos, messages, and matches.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers, and we are fully GDPR compliant. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I report or block a user?

Open the user's profile and tap the menu icon (three dots). You'll see options to Block or Report the user. Blocked users cannot see you or contact you.

What transit lines are available?

Ringos supports S-Bahn (S1-S9, S41, S42, etc.), U-Bahn (U1-U9), and Tram lines (M1, M2, M4, M5, M10, M13, etc.). More cities coming soon!

Why can't I see anyone on my ride?

Other users need to have selected the same line, station, and departure time as you. Try different departure times or check during peak commute hours for more matches!

How do I change my profile photo?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Edit Profile. Tap on your current photo to upload a new one from your camera or photo library.
